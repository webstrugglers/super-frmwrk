OUR STORY

Biska INC is a transport company specializing in general freight transportation, using 53ft Dry Van shipping trailers.

With over 10 years of experience in the industry, we guarantee your freight is in safe hands.

We began our business in Illinois, later we expanded our service to the whole USA and Canada. With our several years of experience, we offer our clients standard, special, and express services.

OUR MISSION

We have focused on raising customer service to the highest possible level.

Our dispatch service is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, and we are always prepared to answer all your questions.

All trucks we offer are new generation which go through inspection before each haul to make sure that everything will be safe and secure.

We are very proud of our drivers, who have been carefully selected based on their experience and high qualifications for the job who make sure the freight is safe at all times.

If you should choose our company, rest assured that you have a reliable and trustworthy partner who at all times fulfills all safety and logistic requirements, which is precisely why we have been in business for over 10 years.
